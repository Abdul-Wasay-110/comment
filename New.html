verb is a word or group of words that expresses an action, an event, or a state of being. It's the part of a sentence that tells you what the subject is doing, experiencing, or how it is. For example, in the sentence "The dog barks," "barks" is the verb indicating an action, while in "The dog is tired," "is" shows a state of being.
